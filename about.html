Our Story

Crown Bicycle Shop established during the early 1990 is a popular business which has developed over the years into a medium sized business, the Crown Bicycle Shop carries out sales of imported branded bikes and also spare parts. we provide the latest stylish and contemporary designs which lets our customers obtain maximum adventure and outstanding experience with a wide range of bicycles for various age groups. we give importance to our customers safety and provide undeniable quality bicycles.

Vision

To make people live healthier and happier lives by contributing to their passion with great enthusiasm and showing them how beautiful life can be and experiences in our lives are with the help of the two wheeled man-made machine.

Mission

To let people explore their passion with enthusiasm through the help of a bicycle.
